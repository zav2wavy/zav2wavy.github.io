About Me

Jo initially studied Fine Arts, in Theatre and Drama faculty, in Greece. She always wanted to step into makeup artistry, so she went to a makeup school and very soon realised that beauty makeup is what she will do.

“I never imagined doing anything else but making women beautifull”

At her early twenties she started working in luxury departments, as a senior makeup artist at MAC Cosmetics. After few months she got a job as a regional makeup artist for YSL Beauty while she was freelancing for weddings and events. “These years really helped me to understand women’s needs in beauty and become more experienced with various skin conditions.”

She decided to move in London at 2018, where she continued her career as a head makeup artist for YSL Beauty at Harrods department. Her creative side led her to take a course at London College of Fashion in creative direction and editorial styling . Ever since she has been working in editorial shoots, collaborating with various stylists as well as taking over clients for special events and weddings across London and in her homeland, Greece.
